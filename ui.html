Internet Radio UI

User Interfaces | 2017

Challenge

The present project aims at designing an audio content reproduction product, as an internet signal receiver, wired and wireless.

This product will it is addressed to adults with spiritual culture and musical culture that they dedicate time from their everyday life to listen to music in their living room.

Evaluation

Users are browsing device to discover the utility and the properties of the individual their characteristics.

Final Conclusions

Initially, when users interact with the device, problems with managing favorites, entering and deleting a station have been identified. In addition, users have made remarkable remarks about the minor difficulties they encountered, such as reading and understanding symbols and letters, the use of buffering as well as the reading distance of its display device.

But then they succeeded in successfully interacting with the device and carrying it on elementary tasks, following some changes that followed (annotations, font size, hierarchy smart color data, larger screen size). Finally, by collecting data from assessing the radio and incorporating it into our final project, the improvement was felt in both external appearance and form as well as in u.i.
