Our Challenge

We design a service based on experience sharing new parents from the ederly. Our goal is creative activation of the elderly and to help young people parents to fulfill their new tasks successfully.

Branding

Vision: Making use of all the wealth of the experience of the elderly through counseling to achieve people's goals and dreams

Value proposition: Making use of past experience marks the course of the future

Fame: Reliable service, sharing experiences, two-way communication environment.
