Water Flask For Snorkeling

Product Design | 2018

Our Challenge

Design a product containing one liter of drinking water which will moisturize the user while navigating the sea without being burdened by snorkeling.

Still, it will take drinking water and will carry it while at the same time it will protect it from external factors (sun, temperature, sea). The product will also have a secondary function as a buoy.

- Preparation

The user fills the water bag of the buoy with water. Closes and simultaneously seals the container. From the special recess, at the bottom of the buoy he presses two liters of air into the surrounding area from the bag water.

The pump used is universal and the recess is compatible with almost all existing ones. The an object connected to the user means of a rope that joins the bottom of the buoy. The point of attachment of the buoy with the user is at the discretion of the user, with possible variations in the foot, hand or waist. The item is ready for use.

Evolution stages

The primary stage for our final concept was "the obvious" use of trapped air (balloon, lifejacket, etc.) to contain water on the surface of the sea. Then, in order to facilitate - reduce the difficulty of using when drinking water, we added to our object the way of water with compressed air.

At the same time, we wanted to give a second function to our final product, helping the user, partial support during the action (how much water).

Next Stage

What has been achieved by shaping such a formula helps the user to stabilize while drinking water.

Then the next problem we encountered was the correct floating of the container-object where we should have no stability problems that had to do with the proper operation of the container and any difficulty that would add to it during the snorkelling activity.

Usage procedure

The user rests on the buoy in the circular cylinder to float partially. To use the container and to drink water, control the container from the three handles (by selecting one or two handles to guide it) that lift the container in the air (and at the highest point of the object).

The user slightly presses his tongue, with his mouth closed, at the mouth of the container. Water is supplied to the user for as long as he presses the nozzle.

Cleaning process

The container, when it has been emptied of all water, contains one liter of air. Although it contains this pressure it iseasy to unscrew with dry hands, so disassemble the container and clean it by cleaning the parts individually.

There is no need to clean the plastic. Thus, the parts that need cleaning are the bag containing the water, and the container mouth.
